BODRUM ORAK ISLAND BOAT TRIP

With the Bodrum Orak Island Boat tour, we offer you a tour opportunity that includes more than a boat tour, and you will be in touch with nature. You can be sure that this island, where green and blue meet, will completely relieve your stress and have an incredible daily excursion in Bodrum. You can swim on the beautiful beaches of this island surrounded by olive trees, discover virgin nature with your family or friends and experience an unforgettable Bodrum daily tour. In this popular tour among Bodrum boat tours, you can visit Orak Island, known as Bodrum's Maldives, by boat and swim and sunbathe during the tour.

Fantastic Bays on Our Route

After picking you up from the hotel at the specified time with our comfortable vehicles, we drive towards Halikarnas Port. Here you are greeted by our professional crew and settle in our boat. During our Boat trip in Bodrum, which will last about 7 hours, offers the opportunity to swim and sunbathe in beautiful bays, especially on Orak Island.

You can sit and sunbathe from the shaded area on the lower floor of our boat throughout the journey, or you can sunbathe on the sunbed upstairs. Since we will spend a lot of time on the ship and sea, we advise you to bring your sun cream, towel and sunglasses. For more clues, you can review the "What should you bring for the Bodrum Orak Island Boat Tour?" section at the bottom of the page.

CORONA (COVID 19) PRECAUTIONS

We are in one of the strangest periods of the century we live in. While we want to experience all these beautiful tours and get rid of the fatigue of the year, we have to be protected from the Coronavirus. Our company takes its own responsibility in this regard and ensures your safety at the maximum level. We have to take some precautions after the daily boat tour program begins. You should know that all of these precautions are taken for the health of you and your loved ones.

All of our boats are regularly disinfected and thoroughly cleaned after each trip. Before getting on the boat, a temperature measurement is made. Attention is very carefully paid to social distance during the boat tour and necessary precautions are taken.

Distant seating arrangements are determined on our boats, and our daily boat tours are realized with 50% capacity. All boats have disinfectant and necessary equipment.

What you can expect during your visit

  • Face masks required for travellers in public areas
  • Face masks required for guides in public areas
  • Hand sanitiser available to travellers and staff
  • Social distancing enforced throughout the experience
  • Regularly sanitized high-traffic areas

* Remember, the health of all of us is important equally
